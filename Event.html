About Us

Civil Engineering Association (CEA) is one of the largest students run organisations in India which is an amalgamation of students, scholars and faculty of Civil Engineering department, IIT Madras. Formed with the primary aim of exposing potential civil engineers to the challenges of the profession, CEA actively encourages the future engineers to celebrate the "joy of engineering". The association conducts workshops, professional events, sports events, along with social campaigns and lectures by great personalities to enhance the all-round development of students throughout the year. Along with the yearlong activities, CEA hosts annually a 3-day long CEA Fest which is a culmination of engineering competitions and workshops, serving as a launching platform for budding civil engineers from all over India.

CEA INAUGURAL

The Inaugural is the introductory event of CEA and it gives an amazing start. It will be usually held in IC&SR auditorium and will be hosted by Convenors of CEA and the Head of the Department(HoD) , also along with the CEA Secretaries and Student Advisers of the respective year.The Chief guest shares his/her valuable experiences.Later, the CEA Theme and the tentative schedule of CEA Activities for the corresponding year will be announced.Generally, CEA Inaugural will be organised by CEA Nirmaan Team, but some events during the inaugural like Crossword puzzles, will be conducted by CEA.

SUSTAINABLE DESIGN CHALLENGE

The greatest threat to our planet is the belief that someone else will save it.” The CEA sustainable design challenge provides a platform for students to design efficient and self sustainable infrastructure and systems that are more environmentally friendly. Participants are asked to design sustainable residential buildings of single storey and calculate the life cycle energy use. The evaluation parameters include cost of construction, materials chosen, the details provided to each life cycle phase, the overall cost of the building and life cycle energy of the building.

AQUANOMICS

Aquanomics comprehensively examines the full range of water problems and reveals measures that should be implemented to avoid the onset of possible “water crisis”. Aquanomics is an event where participants use their hydraulic and water resources engineering concepts to solve an ecological issue.This event will be conducted for two days, wherein the first day, a software workshop will be held for the participants and on the second day, they apply their practical and software skills to model, simulate and find an efficient solution to a real-life water related problem.

GEO GENIUS

This is a flagship event of the Geotechnical department of civil engineering. In most of the building failure cases, the root cause is ‘poor foundation’. Whether it is an earthquake or a hurricane, it is the foundation geology that decides the future of structure. In this edition of Geo Genius, participants are required to construct an embankment using recycled materials thereby enriching cost efficiency and solving the problem of waste disposal.

CONCRETE CHALLENGE

In today’s world, Concrete has become a very essential construction material and is used for constructing many famous skyscrapers, some of which include “Burj Khalifa” and “Petronas Twin Towers”. In CEA Fest 2020 participants were asked to prepare a concrete frame using steel fibre reinforced concrete (SFRC).Later, it is tested for its compressive strength and ultimate load capacity in flexure. The competition has always been offering participants a chance to explore and learn more of concrete technology.

MASTER BUILDER

Master Builder is an event in which participants will have to build a miniature model of a superstructure with given materials and constraints.The model is then tested for its axial load bearing capacity and load/weight ratio. Sometimes the model goes through vigorous load tests like earthquake simulation and high-speed wind tests. The model that withstands these ultimate testing conditions at its best with given constraints is considered the winner. The participants get a good dose of practical learning to complement their strong fundamentals.

PROJECT X (Modeling)

As the name suggests, modeling events require the participants to create a three-dimensional structure with the materials provided within a given time. This event provides a perfect competitive platform for all those who have an unexplained passion for building structures. Prerequisites required are basic knowledge of civil engineering especially structural engineering. The winners will be decided based on load bearing capacity, and aesthetics of the model made with the effective use of materials provided.Project X is the similar version of Modeling, but will be conducted during the semester time.

ENCODE STEEL

This event is a real test of participants' grey matter, designing skills and their creativity as a civil engineer. In this event participants will be asked to design a residential structure which includes designing of reinforced concrete slabs, beams, columns and corresponding steel reinforcements for a given loading and also with clear cost analysis of structure. With a huge participation over the past few years, this event has been recognized as one of the best events in CEA.

BON-AUTOROUTIER

In recent years, traffic congestion has become a major problem in many parts of the world. One way to alleviate congestion is to manage the traffic efficiently by Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS). In this event, participants come up with a signal timing strategy for two intersections of six-lane and four-lane, to reduce the average delay for commuters. This event provides a wonderful opportunity to students, making them think like a transport designer to find efficient solutions for an existing traffic plight and enhance their planning ability.

CASE STUDY

Case studies are the best practical exercise of your grey cells which makes use of all your mental faculties as well as your decision-making skills. These are one of the best teaching tools for enhancing the practical knowledge of students. In this event, the participants are required to solve failure case studies with a detailed analysis of a pre-chosen civil structure and present it in the form of a PowerPoint presentation. They use their problem-solving skills to come up with the solutions to the given problem. We believe that young ideas have the power to bring about the required change in the society.
